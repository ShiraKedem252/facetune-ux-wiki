# Source: Editing Needs — Existing vs. New Users

**Researcher:** Shira | **Date:** November 2025 | **Sample:** n=835 survey (Facetune users vs. non-users)

Lighting is the #1 editing need universally — both Facetune users and non-users cite it as their primary frustration. However, skin smoothing is Facetune's core differentiator: Millennials using Facetune rate skin smoothing **27.6 percentage points higher** than Millennials not using Facetune.

## Universal Need: Lighting

| User Group | "Lighting is my #1 editing problem" |
|---|---|
| Facetune users | 89% |
| Non-Facetune users | 87% |
| Gen Z | 84% |
| Millennials | 91% |

Lighting fixes transcend all user segments and app preferences. Users want to:
- Brighten underexposed photos
- Reduce shadows on faces
- Warm up cool-toned images
- Enhance outdoor natural light

**Strategic implication:** Lighting tools should be prominent, easy to access, and high-quality. This is table stakes across the photo editing category.

## The Skin Smoothing Differentiator

Facetune's competitive advantage: AI-powered skin smoothing at mobile speed and quality.

| Segment | Facetune Users | Non-Facetune Users | Difference |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Millennials** | 76.2% prioritize skin smoothing | 48.6% | **+27.6 pts** |
| **Gen Z** | 62.1% | 58.3% | +3.8 pts |
| **All users** | 71.4% | 54.2% | +17.2 pts |

The gap is **biggest for Millennials** — Facetune users over-index dramatically on skin smoothing importance. This aligns with [[persona-nicole-beauty-driven-millennial]] being the largest segment (47%).

**Why the generational difference?**
- **Millennials:** Grew up with Instagram; skin editing is cultural norm
- **Gen Z:** More authenticity-conscious; skin smoothing feels less important (but still desired)

## Segment Alignment

[[persona-nicole-beauty-driven-millennial]] drives the skin smoothing adoption — she's the reason Facetune users rate skin smoothing 27.6 points higher.

[[persona-zoe-gen-z-selective]] pulls down Gen Z averages — she edits less, questions authenticity, but still occasionally uses skin smoothing.

## Methodology

- **Type:** Quantitative survey
- **Participants:** 835 total (Facetune users vs. non-users; split by generation)
- **Measures:** Importance ratings for 10+ editing needs
- **Date:** November 2025
