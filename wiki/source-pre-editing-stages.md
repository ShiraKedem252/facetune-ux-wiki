# Source: Pre-Editing Stages & Photo Selection

**Researcher:** Shira | **Date:** (date from study) | **Sample:** n= (from study)

Photo selection is the most time-consuming stage in the editing workflow — users spend **15-50 minutes** evaluating which image to edit. The tension between realism and AI accuracy directly impacts selection decisions. Retake is most useful when users like specific aspects of a photo but want to improve others.

## The Time Investment Problem

Users spend disproportionate time on **selection** vs. **editing**:

| Stage | Time Spent | User Frustration |
|-------|---|---|
| Selection (choosing which photo) | 15-50 min | High (indecision, perfectionism) |
| Editing (actually retouching) | 2-5 min | Low (once in app) |
| Approval (deciding if done) | 5-10 min | Medium (quality checking) |

This reveals an opportunity: tools that **speed photo selection** or **reduce selection anxiety** would have high adoption.

## Photo Selection Criteria

Users filter photos by:
1. **Lighting** — Does it have good natural light?
2. **Expression** — Am I making the right face?
3. **Angle** — Is my best angle captured?
4. **Proportions** — Does my body look right?
5. **Background** — Is the setting acceptable?

Multiple decision points create decision paralysis, especially for [[persona-annie-memory-documenter]] and [[persona-rachel-family-photographer]].

## Key AI Accuracy Insights

Users identified these inaccuracies in AI editing:

- **Realism gaps** — AI-enhanced image looks "off" or over-processed
- **Feature inconsistencies** — Skin texture fine in some areas, blotchy in others
- **Lighting artifacts** — Shadows don't match source lighting direction
- **Expression changes** — AI subtly alters smile, eye direction, or mood

These inaccuracies make users reluctant to commit to AI output without manual review.

## Retake Use Case

Retake gains traction when users have **one great shot with specific imperfections**:
- "This expression is perfect, but lighting is bad" → Retake helps
- "I love my pose, but the background is distracting" → Retake helps
- "My skin looks great in this but my smile is off" → Retake helps

Retake fails when the entire photo is problematic — users prefer to re-shoot or select a different image.

## Methodology

- **Type:** Qualitative study + early insights synthesis
- **Methods:** Shadowing users as they selected photos to edit, think-aloud protocol
- **Coverage:** Mix of phone users and desktop users
